# Support

We are here to help! If you encounter issues while using WiBLE, you have several ways to get support:

## Community Support

- **Discord**: Join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/wible) to chat with other users and the maintainers.
- **GitHub Discussions**: Ask questions, share ideas, and get help in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Chamath-Adithya/WiBLE/discussions).

## Bug Reports and Feature Requests

If you found a bug or want to request a new feature, please open an issue on our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Chamath-Adithya/WiBLE/issues) page. Please ensure you use the appropriate issue template and provide as much detail as possible.

## Commercial Support

For enterprise users requiring guaranteed response times, custom feature development, or integration assistance, please contact us directly at achamth1@gmail.com.
